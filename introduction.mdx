# Welcome to Dazense

Dazense is an open-source analytics agent that helps teams explore and answer business questions by combining data, metadata, and business context into a governed experience.

## What you can do

- Chat with your data using natural language.
- Govern metrics with a semantic layer.
- Enforce critical business rules automatically.
- Sync databases, repos, and docs into context.
- Self-host securely with your own LLM keys.
